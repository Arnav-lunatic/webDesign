Color Theory

Color theory is a field of study that explores how colors interact with one another and how they can be combined to create visually pleasing compositions. It involves understanding concepts such as color harmony, contrast, and the psychological effects of different colors. Color theory is widely used in various disciplines, including art, design, and marketing, to create effective visual communication and evoke specific emotions or responses. By understanding color theory, one can make informed decisions about color palettes, color combinations, and overall visual aesthetics.

Complementary Color The Colors which are opposite to each other. The combination of them can create that pop. But Don't do this it with text because it make the text hard to read.

What is White Space:-
When it comes to white space in web design, it refers to the empty spaces between elements on a webpage. White space is important as it helps improve readability, organization, and visual clarity. It allows the content to breathe and creates a balanced and uncluttered layout.

The Apple MacBook site demonstrates how effectively it uses white space on a page.
